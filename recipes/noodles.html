Noodles

Buttered noodles are a simple dish to make.

They require little prep work and are great for picky eaters.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Add the noodles and cook to pack directions
  3. Once cooked, drain the noodles and add to a bowl
  4. Add butter and seasonings to taste
