Katsu Curry

Chicken Katsu Curry Recipe

Katsu curry originates from Japan.

It is different from other curries that you may have tried, like Indian curries for example. Japanese curries use different spices, and usually start with a roux - making them taste unlike others.

Ingredients

  • S&B Golden Curry block
  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Start cooking the rice
  2. Sweat the veg
  3. Add water to the veg and bring to boil
  4. Simmer veg for 15 mins
  5. Add curry blocks
